#ICDMAIN: ICD-10 main chapters

[1] ICDMAIN_ANY_ENTRY: Any ICDMAIN event in hilmo or causes of death (ICD-10: [ABCEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ]|D[0-8])
 [2] I_INFECT_PARASIT: Certain infectious and parasitic diseases (ICD-10: A|B)
 [2] II_NEOPLASM: Neoplasms (ICD-10: C|D[0-4])
 [2] III_BLOOD_IMMUN: Diseases of the blood and blood-forming organs and certain disorders involving the immune mechanism (ICD-10: D[5-8])
 [2] IV_ENDOCRIN_NUTRIT: Endocrine, nutritional and metabolic diseases (ICD-10: E)
 [2] V_MENTAL_BEHAV: Mental and behavioural disorders (ICD-10: F)
 [2] VI_NERVOUS: Diseases of the nervous system (ICD-10: G)
 [2] VII_EYE_ADNEXA: Diseases of the eye and adnexa (ICD-10: H[0-5])
 [2] VIII_EAR_MASTOID: Diseases of the ear and mastoid process (ICD-10: H[6-9])
 [2] IX_CIRCULATORY: Diseases of the circulatory system (ICD-10: I)
 [2] X_RESPIRATORY: Diseases of the respiratory system (ICD-10: J)
 [2] XI_DIGESTIVE: Diseases of the digestive system (ICD-10: K)
 [2] XII_SKIN_SUBCUTAN: Diseases of the skin and subcutaneous tissue (ICD-10: L)
 [2] XIII_MUSCULOSKELET: Diseases of the musculoskeletal system and connective tissue (ICD-10: M)
 [2] XIV_GENITOURINARY: Diseases of the genitourinary system (ICD-10: N)
 [2] XV_PREGNANCY_BIRTH: Pregnancy, childbirth and the puerperium (ICD-10: O)
 [2] XVI_PERINATAL: Certain conditions originating in the perinatal period (ICD-10: P)
 [2] XVII_MALFORMAT_ABNORMAL: Congenital malformations, deformations and chromosomal abnormalities (ICD-10: Q)
 [2] XVIII_MISCFINDINGS: Symptoms, signs and abnormal clinical and laboratory findings, not elsewhere classified (ICD-10: R)
 [2] XIX_INJURY_POISON: Injury, poisoning and certain other consequences of external causes (ICD-10: S|T)
 [2] XX_EXTERNAL_MORB_MORT: External causes of morbidity and mortality (ICD-10: V|W|X|Y)
 [2] XXI_HEALTHFACTORS: Factors influencing health status and contact with health services (ICD-10: Z)
 [2] XXII_SPECIAL: Codes for special purpose (ICD-10: U)
